Parrott Plaza is a well-established mixed-use commercial center positioned at the heart of Savannah's southside commercial spine. Located on Montgomery Cross Road just east of Abercorn Street, the center benefits from strong daily traffic, excellent surface parking, and a diverse tenant mix that draws consistent foot traffic throughout the week.

Three office suites are currently available on the second floor, each suited to a different occupier profile. Suite P offers a spacious 1,900 SF layout with a reception area, multiple private offices, and a bullpen — ideal for professional services firms, regional sales offices, or back-office operations. Suite H provides a compact 1,100 SF configuration. Suite K, located in the front building with ADA elevator access, features high ceilings, wide common halls, and excellent natural light.

The center sits in a dense commercial node surrounded by national anchors, new hospitality development, and the transformative Oglethorpe Mall mixed-use redevelopment. With Savannah's Non-CBD office vacancy at just 4.5% and average asking rents at $34.97/SF/yr, Parrott Plaza's rates represent a compelling value gap for tenants seeking quality space without premium pricing. Suite H is configured for storage or warehouse use — ideal for businesses needing flexible back-office or inventory space alongside professional office operations.

Why This Deal Wins

Four data-backed reasons to
lease at Parrott Plaza now.

Savannah's Non-CBD office market is tightening. Here is why Parrott Plaza represents the strongest value play on the southside today.

01
65% Below Market — Suite P at $12.00/SF vs. $34.97/SF Market Average
Cushman & Wakefield's Q3 2025 MarketBeat confirms Savannah's Non-CBD average asking rent at $34.97/SF/yr. Suite P's $12.00/SF/yr rate represents a 65.7% discount to market — the widest value gap available in the Southside submarket for a professionally configured office suite. Tenants who lock in today are insulated from the rent escalation trend that has pushed effective rates upward for six consecutive quarters.
02
Tightest Submarket in Savannah — 4.5% Non-CBD Vacancy Leaves Almost No Alternatives
With Non-CBD vacancy at 4.5% (Q3 2025, Cushman & Wakefield), comparable office options in the Southside corridor are nearly exhausted. Parrott Plaza's three available suites represent an immediate, no-build-out solution in a market where the next closest Class B alternative is priced at $20–$30/SF/yr. Tenants who delay risk losing access to the only sub-$20/SF option in the immediate trade area.
03
Oglethorpe Mall Redevelopment Adds Hundreds of Rooftops Within Half a Mile
Madison Communities' conversion of the former Sears anchor at Oglethorpe Mall — located 0.45 miles from Parrott Plaza — is nearing completion, adding a significant residential population to the immediate trade area. This densification, combined with the new Texas Roadhouse directly across the street and the Tru by Hilton Savannah Midtown 0.11 miles away, is accelerating the transformation of the Montgomery Cross Road corridor into one of Savannah's most active commercial nodes.
04
Move-In Ready with Modified Gross Terms — No Hidden Costs, No Build-Out Delay
All three suites are available for immediate occupancy with landlord-provided minor cosmetic improvements. The Modified Gross lease structure provides cost predictability — tenants know their monthly obligation without the uncertainty of triple-net escalations. With $108,956 average household income within the 3-mile trade area and 34,904 residents in ZIP 31406, the consumer base supporting Parrott Plaza's tenant mix is among the strongest in the Savannah southside market.
