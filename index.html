Parrott Plaza is a well-established mixed-use commercial center positioned at the heart of Savannah's southside commercial spine. Located on Montgomery Cross Road just east of Abercorn Street, the center benefits from strong daily traffic, excellent surface parking, and a diverse tenant mix that draws consistent foot traffic throughout the week.

Three suites are currently available, each suited to a different occupier profile: a 1,900 SF second-floor office suite ideal for professional services, a 1,100 SF office suite with a private restroom, and a 1,100 SF ground-level flex/storage unit with an overhead drive-in rollup door — a rare and highly functional configuration for distribution, contractor storage, or light industrial use.

The center sits in a dense commercial node surrounded by national and regional retailers, a newly opened Roadhouse Grill directly across the street, a recently developed hotel, and the ongoing Oglethorpe Mall mixed-use redevelopment — all driving increased daytime and evening traffic to the corridor.
